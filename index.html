About me

Hi, my name is Marcin, and here are a few words about me. I’ve been working with code – building mobile apps, websites, and backend solutions. I enjoy challenges and never shy away from them – I believe there’s no such thing as impossible, only things I haven’t done yet. I’m a creative person driven by the process of building things. That process is my fuel – it keeps me going and brings me the most satisfaction. I care not only about how things work, but also how they look – I like to create things that are both functional and beautiful. Pinterest is my second home – I’m constantly looking for inspiration, and discovering new ideas is a true passion of mine. Whether it’s design, user experience, or creative things, I love exploring what’s possible and pushing my own boundaries.

Mobile apps

I have worked on mobile application projects using both native iOS technologies (Swift, SwiftUI) and the cross-platform Flutter framework. My experience includes both commercial and personal projects — from early MVPs to production-ready apps. I’ve contributed to applications for e-commerce platforms, loyalty programs, and social media, always with a strong focus on performance, usability, and polished, user-friendly interfaces. I also have hands-on experience in designing UI and UX — this area is far from unfamiliar to me. Crafting thoughtful, intuitive interfaces is just as important to me as writing clean, maintainable code. I care deeply about how an app works and how it feels. ⸻

I create modern websites — from simple business pages to complex web platforms. I have experience setting up WooCommerce stores and a strong command of WordPress. In every project, I focus on aesthetics, functionality, and performance.

The podcast is a space where I share my passion for the automotive world. I talk to interesting guests and present their stories, which are especially inspiring for those who are passionate about creating within the automotive field. This project was born out of a desire to share knowledge and experience, as well as to build a community around shared interests.

Photography

Automotive Photography

I’m fascinated by the world of automotive design – I love capturing details, lines, and the emotions connected to cars. Automotive photography is, for me, a blend of passion for technology, light, and motion. I aim to show not just the look, but also the character of the machines and the atmosphere of automotive events.

Street Photography

Street photography allows me to capture everyday city life, people’s emotions, and unique moments. I enjoy observing the world around me and looking for interesting frames that tell stories without words.
